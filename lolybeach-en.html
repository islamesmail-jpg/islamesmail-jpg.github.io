About Loly Beach, Ain Sokhna

DNA for Engineering & Real Estate Development launched “Loly Beach” as an upscale architectural landmark in Ain Sokhna, with a mild climate and stunning sea views. It offers modern, high-quality units packed with amenities, at competitive prices and flexible payment plans that encourage investment.

Location

Located in the heart of Ain Sokhna near service hubs and main axes; right off Zafarana Road, 3 km from the checkpoint and 8 km from the Sokhna Gate. It’s also close to Mövenpick Hotel and “Ain” village, with direct views of the Red Sea.

Why Loly Beach

  • Prime, services-rich location that boosts demand.
  • Strong infrastructure and integrated services.
  • Luxury units with direct views of the surrounding nature.
  • Expansive landscape and gardens for a calm, breezy feel.
  • Crystal lagoons and dancing fountains with standout designs.
  • Advanced security systems, 24/7 guards, and e-gates.
  • Pools in varied lengths and depths for all ages.
  • High-efficiency central Wi-Fi inside units.
  • Covered, secure parking to preserve the elegant look.

Amenities & Services

  • Aqua park and water activities for all ages.
  • Luxury hotel with premium hospitality services.
  • Golf course and golf carts for mobility.
  • Equipped gym with professional trainers, and spa (sauna/massage).
  • Hospitals & 24/7 pharmacies, emergency rooms.
  • Entertainment island with diverse experiences.
  • Diving & fishing centers, and a marina for yachts.
  • Walking/jogging tracks and yoga zones.
  • Meeting and events halls.
  • Sports club and courts (football/tennis/basketball).
  • Mosques and worship spaces.
  • Shopping mall with services and international brands.
  • Restaurants & cafés, hypermarket, and delivery.
  • High-quality cinemas.
  • Ongoing maintenance throughout the week.

Developer & Track Record

DMA is among Egypt’s leading developers, with experience delivering integrated communities in the New Capital, Ain Sokhna, El Shorouk, and Sheikh Zayed, with strong commitment to quality and handover timelines.
