About Loly Beach

DMA Developments launched its latest masterpiece “Loly Beach” in Ain Sokhna with direct views of the Red Sea, modern luxury units, and flexible payment plans encouraging investment.

Location

Located at the Zafarana entrance, only 3 km from the checkpoint and 8 km from Ain Sokhna Gate, near Mövenpick Hotel and “Ain Village”.

Features

  • Prime location close to roads and services.
  • Integrated infrastructure and full facilities.
  • Luxury units with direct sea & landscape views.
  • Wide gardens and green areas.
  • Crystal lagoons & dancing fountains.
  • 24/7 advanced security with electronic gates.
  • Swimming pools of various sizes.
  • High-speed central Wi-Fi.
  • Covered, secure parking garages.

Developer
