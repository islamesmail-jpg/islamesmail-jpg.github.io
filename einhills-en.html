About Ein Hills (Ain Sokhna)

Ein Hills is Al Shahawi Group’s flagship launch in Ain Sokhna, perched on a terraced mountain top with panoramic Red Sea views. The project blends generous green areas and waterfalls with contemporary European façades and smart tech. A full amenities mix and flexible payment plans deliver a fresh coastal lifestyle.

The Developer: Al Shahawi Group

Founded in 1976 with 30+ years of experience, Al Shahawi delivered successful residential and commercial projects and owns leading companies such as the Arabian Cement Company and Al Sukhour Marble.

Selected Al Shahawi Projects

  • Bali — North Coast
  • Mövenpick El Sokhna Hotel

Location

On Galala Plateau in the heart of Ain Sokhna — approx. 127 km from Cairo and 8 km from Galala City — reachable in around 60 minutes.

  • New Administrative Capital: ~47 km (~45 min)
  • Ain Sokhna gates: ~15 km
  • Galala City: 8 km
  • Porto Sokhna: ~15 min
  • New Cairo: ~100 km

Land Area

Total ~50 acres with ~800 m beachfront and ~250 m depth, around 500 units with sea views. The plan features greenery, water features, retail & F&B (~100 m²), crystal lagoons and waterfalls (~4,200 m²).

Design Concept

World-class terraced masterplan so almost all homes enjoy sea views. Modern weather-resistant façades and materials with multiple lagoons and lush landscaping.

Amenities & Features

  • Expansive landscape & artificial lakes
  • Dedicated ladies’ recreation area
  • Restaurants, cafés & a retail zone
  • Health club (sauna/jacuzzi) & gym

  • Aqua park & amusement zone
  • Medical unit & 24/7 pharmacy

  • Yoga & jogging paths
  • Lifeguards on beach & sports courts
  • 5-star hotel — cable car & electric train
  • Mall & backup generators
  • Secure parking, 24/7 security
