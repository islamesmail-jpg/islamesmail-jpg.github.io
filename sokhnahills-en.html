Sokhna Hills — Overview

Sokhna Hills is one of Ain Sokhna’s standout villages developed by Al Hejaz Developments, perched on the Red Sea mountains in the heart of Ain Sokhna. It’s roughly one hour from Cairo and right next to Porto Sokhna. The project spans about 100 acres with a private beach, master-planned so all units enjoy sea views.

Unit mix includes standard chalets, garden chalets, townhouses, and standalone villas — with competitive pricing and long, flexible payment plans.

Location & Nearby Roads

Set on Galala Plateau — ~60 minutes from Cairo, ~10 minutes from Porto Sokhna, and ~30 minutes from the airport.

  • Cairo – Ain Sokhna Road.
  • Katameya – Ain Sokhna Highway.
  • El Kureimat – Zafarana Road.
  • Suez – Ain Sokhna Road.

Architecture & Concept

A fully serviced family entertainment city designed with stepped terraces to ensure 100% sea views, bike & e-scooter lanes, one of Sokhna’s largest aqua parks, a 4-star hotel and cinema, plus generous landscaping, crystal lagoons, and activity zones for all ages.

Components & Areas

  • Total land ~100 acres (the second largest resort on Galala after Il Monte Galala).
  • Private beach ~2,800 m².
  • Unit types: Chalets — Townhouses — Standalone Villas.
  • 4-star hotel directly on the sea with full hospitality services.
  • 24/7 commercial zones (restaurants/cafés/hypermarket).
  • Site elevation ~80 m above sea level for panoramic views.

Amenities & Advantages

  • Smart gates and reception desks.
  • 24/7 security and CCTV coverage.
  • Residents’ internal parking.
  • Large aqua park for adults and kids.
  • Multiple swimming pools (incl. heated), kids’ pool, and covered ladies’ pool.
  • Spa, gym, and children’s play areas.
  • Landscaped gardens and crystal lagoons.
  • Local & international retail brands.
  • On-site medical center & emergency clinic.
  • 4-star hotel with premium services for owners.
