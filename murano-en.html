About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna is one of the most attractive resorts on the Red Sea coast, offering a calm seaside lifestyle with modern services. Its location at the end of Ain Sokhna Road and the beginning of Zafarana Road makes it among the closest beach destinations to Cairo and easily accessible through key highways. The project delivers a complete living experience from the sandy beach and marina to vibrant service areas and homes designed for families.

Location

Murano Wadi Degla sits in a prime spot on the Red Sea, close to Galala Road and Ain Sokhna train station, and within a short driving distance from Cairo and Katameya—saving time and effort on every trip.

Nearby Roads & Landmarks

  • Galala Road: 18 km
  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: 14 km
  • Cairo: 136 km
  • Katameya Road: 112 km
