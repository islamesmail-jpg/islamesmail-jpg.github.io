Project Master Plan

1 — About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna is a serene Red Sea retreat, away from city noise yet within comfortable driving distance. It blends natural beauty with modern luxury. The village is carefully planned to prioritize calm and privacy while delivering complete comfort and entertainment. Its unique location and diverse facilities make it ideal for families. Natural hot springs nearby add a special touch to the overall experience.

2 — Location

Wadi Degla Developments chose a prime spot for Murano at the end of the Ain Sokhna road and the beginning of Zafarana Road — one of the best positions on the Red Sea coast. This makes it one of the closest beach destinations to Cairo and perfectly connected to the country’s main road network for easy access from different governorates.

3 — Key Roads & Distances

  • Galala Road: ~18 km from Murano.
  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: ~14 km.
  • Cairo: ~136 km.
  • Katameya Road: ~112 km.

4 — Site Area & Planning

Wadi Degla allocated a large area for Murano and optimized land use to deliver an excellent final layout, with many leisure and daily-life facilities. The master planning balances privacy and comfort, with generous green zones and water features that create a relaxing atmosphere across the village.

5 — Master Plan & Facilities

The master plan embraces a “nature + lifestyle” concept to deliver a memorable experience in every zone. Facilities include:

  • Swimming pools: a variety of sizes for adults and kids.
  • Kids’ play areas: designed with top safety standards.
  • Restaurants & cafés: diverse, quality dining options.
  • Retail: everyday shopping without leaving the village.
  • Walking & cycling paths: through lush greenery and fresh air.

6 — Snapshot Recap

Murano merges Red Sea charm with contemporary living — and the presence of natural hot springs nearby adds a distinctive wellness appeal.

7 — Location (Short Recap)

At the junction of Ain Sokhna & Zafarana roads, close to core highways, ensuring smooth access from Greater Cairo and beyond.

Its portfolio spans ~5.5 million m² across 16 projects (12 delivered) with 10,000+ units in Cairo, Giza, North Coast, and Ain Sokhna — focusing on family-centric communities, quality infrastructure, services, and generous green spaces.
