About Murano Wadi Degla Project

Murano Ain Sokhna village is a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle of the city, yet not too far, making travel to and from it easy and quick. On the shores of the Red Sea, Murano offers a unique experience that combines the charm of nature with the luxury of modern life. Murano Wadi Degla was carefully designed to provide an exceptional environment focused on tranquility and serenity, while providing all means of comfort and entertainment. It is distinguished by its unique location and diverse facilities, in addition to the presence of natural hot springs that add a distinctive touch to the village.

Murano Ain Sokhna Location

Wadi Degla for Real Estate Development was keen on choosing a highly distinctive location for Murano Wadi Degla. Murano village is located in one of the best areas of the Red Sea coast, at the end of Ain Sokhna road and the beginning of Zafarana road. This location makes it the closest coastal area to the capital, and it is also close to main roads and axes, ensuring easy access from various parts of Egypt.

Key Roads and Axes Near Murano

  • Galala Road is only 18 km from Murano Sokhna village
  • The distance to Ain Sokhna train station is 14 km
  • The distance between Murano Ain Sokhna village and Cairo is 136 km
  • Katameya Road is 112 km away
