1 — About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna is a serene getaway away from city noise yet close enough for quick trips. On the Red Sea shores, Murano delivers a unique blend of nature and modern comfort. Murano Wadi Degla is carefully designed to offer a calm, tranquil environment with comprehensive leisure and daily comforts. It enjoys a prime location and rich facilities, plus natural hot springs that add a special touch to the village.

2 — Location of Murano Ain Sokhna

Wadi Degla Developments selected a highly strategic location for Murano Wadi Degla. The project sits at one of the best spots on the Red Sea coast, at the end of Ain Sokhna Road and the beginning of Zafarana Road. This makes it the closest seaside area to the capital and keeps it near main roads and axes for easy access from across Egypt.

3 — Key roads & hubs near Murano

  • Ain Sokhna train station: 14 km.
  • Cairo: 136 km.
  • Katameya Road: 112 km.

4 — Project size

Wadi Degla allocated a generous area for Murano Ain Sokhna and optimized the distribution to get the best result, while integrating many leisure and service amenities that create a unique residential experience. The large site and smart planning of Murano Sokhna balance privacy and comfort with abundant greenery and beautiful water features for a soothing vibe.
