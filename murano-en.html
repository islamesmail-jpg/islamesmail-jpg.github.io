Project Master Plan

1 — About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna is a serene Red Sea retreat away from city noise yet within easy reach of Cairo. The project blends natural charm with modern comfort, crafted to offer calm living with complete convenience and entertainment. Its standout location and varied facilities are complemented by natural hot springs that add a unique touch to the community.

2 — Prime Location

Murano Wadi Degla sits at the end of Ain Sokhna Road and the start of Zafarana Road, making it one of the closest coastal destinations to the capital and easily accessible via major routes.

3 — Key Nearby Roads & Landmarks

  • Galala Road: ~18 km
  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: ~14 km
  • Cairo distance: ~136 km
  • Katameya Road: ~112 km

4 — Site Area & Landscape

A generously planned site with smart land use, creating a residential environment that balances privacy and comfort, with ample greenery, water features, and a rich mix of leisure amenities.

5 — Master Plan Highlights

  • Restaurants & cafés
  • Retail for daily needs
  • Walking & cycling paths through green zones

6 — Unit Types

Multiple sizes to suit different needs and budgets, with high-quality finishing for a superior living experience.

7 — Facilities

8 — About Wadi Degla Developments

Established in 2005, Wadi Degla Developments has become a leading Egyptian developer with a portfolio totaling ~5.5 million m², including 16 projects (with 12 delivered) and over 10,000 units across Cairo, Giza, North Coast, and Ain Sokhna.
