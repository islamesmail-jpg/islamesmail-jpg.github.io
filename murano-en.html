About Murano

Murano Wadi Degla is one of the most attractive resorts in Ain Sokhna, combining sandy beaches, a private marina, hotel, pools, and family-friendly units. Its location makes it one of the closest resorts to Cairo.

Location

Located directly on the Red Sea, near Galala Road and Sokhna train station, just 90 minutes from Cairo.

Facilities

  • 750m sandy beach
  • 100m marina
  • 4-star hotel
  • Restaurants & cafés
  • Pools
