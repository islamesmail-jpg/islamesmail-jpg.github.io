Project Master Plan

1 — About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna is a peaceful escape from the city bustle while still being close enough for quick, easy trips. On the shores of the Red Sea, Murano blends nature’s charm with contemporary comfort. The community is carefully designed to prioritize calm and privacy while offering all essentials and leisure amenities. Its prime location, diverse facilities, and even natural hot springs make Murano truly distinctive.

2 — Location

Murano Wadi Degla sits at the end of the Sokhna Road and the start of Zafarana Road, making it one of the closest seaside destinations to Cairo, with excellent access to major highways and transport links.

3 — Key Roads & Distances

  • Al Galala Road: approx. 18 km.
  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: around 14 km.
  • Cairo: about 136 km.
  • Katameya Road: roughly 112 km.

4 — Site Area & Layout

The master plan allocates generous green areas and water features, with smart circulation and spacing that achieve privacy, comfort, and a calm resort feel—alongside plenty of amenities and entertainment options.

5 — Master Plan Highlights

  • Swimming pools in various sizes.
  • Children’s play areas with high safety standards.
  • Restaurants & cafés with diverse options.
  • Retail for daily needs.
  • Walkways & cycling paths through green zones.

6 — Unit Types

  • Apartments
  • Villas

Both apartments and villas come in multiple sizes to suit different needs and budgets, with quality finishes and a refined coastal lifestyle.

7 — Amenities

  • Sandy private beach around 750 m long.
  • Marina approximately 100 m.
  • 4-star hotel within the resort.
  • Commercial area, spa, and beach sports.
  • Natural hot springs.

8 — About Wadi Degla Developments

Founded in 2005, Wadi Degla Developments is among Egypt’s prominent developers with a portfolio of about 5.5 million m², including 16 projects (of which 12 are delivered) and over 10,000 units across Cairo, Giza, the North Coast, and Ain Sokhna.
