Project Master Plan

1 — About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna is a calm seaside escape away from the city rush yet close enough for quick weekend getaways. On the Red Sea shores, Murano blends natural beauty with modern comfort. The project is carefully designed to offer a serene atmosphere with all conveniences and entertainment, a prime location, diverse facilities, and even natural hot springs that add a unique touch to the village.

2 — Location

Wadi Degla chose a highly strategic spot for Murano: at the end of Ain Sokhna Road and the start of Zafarana Road. It’s the closest Red Sea destination to the capital and is connected to key highways, ensuring easy access from all over Egypt.

3 — Nearby Roads & Distances

  • El Galala Road: ~18 km.
  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: ~14 km.
  • Cairo: ~136 km.
  • Katameya Road: ~112 km.

4 — Project Land Area

Murano spans a generous land area thoughtfully planned to maximize usability and lifestyle quality. The wide layout hosts multiple leisure facilities providing a unique residential experience, with privacy, comfort, generous green spaces and water features that create a tranquil vibe.

5 — Master Plan Highlights

The master plan fuses luxury with nature to deliver a distinctive everyday experience. Facilities include:

  • Swimming pools: various sizes for adults and kids.
  • Kids’ areas with top safety standards.
  • Restaurants & cafés with diverse cuisines.
  • Retail for daily needs within the village.
  • Walking & cycling paths amid landscaped greenery.

6 — Quick Summary

Murano combines nature and modern lifestyle, enhanced by natural hot springs within the destination.
