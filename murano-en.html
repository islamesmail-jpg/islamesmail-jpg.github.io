Murano Wadi Degla | Ain Sokhna

Project Video

1- About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna offers a serene getaway from the city while remaining close enough for quick trips. Set on the Red Sea, it blends natural beauty with modern comfort. Murano Wadi Degla is thoughtfully designed to create a calm, relaxing atmosphere with comprehensive amenities. A distinctive highlight is the presence of natural hot springs nearby.

2- Location

The project sits at the end of Ain Sokhna Road and the beginning of Zafarana Road—one of the closest coastal destinations to Cairo—offering excellent accessibility via major highways and corridors.

3- Distances & Access

  • El Galala Road: 18 km.
  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: 14 km.
  • Cairo: approx. 136 km.
  • Katameya Road: 112 km.

4- Project Area & Planning

The masterplan dedicates generous land to green areas and water features, delivering privacy and comfort. The layout supports a relaxed lifestyle with abundant recreational facilities.

5- Master Plan Highlights

  • Multiple swimming pools of various sizes.
  • Safe kids’ play areas.
  • Premium restaurants & cafes.
  • Retail for daily needs.
  • Walking & cycling lanes among landscaped greenery.

6- Units

Residential options include Apartments and Villas with a wide range of sizes and high-quality finishes to suit different preferences and budgets.

7- Amenities

  • Private sandy beach: ~750 m.
  • Marina: ~100 m.
  • 4-star hotel.
  • Retail zone, spa, and beach sports courts.
  • Natural hot springs.

8- About Wadi Degla Developments

Established in 2005, Wadi Degla Developments has built a strong portfolio across Cairo, Giza, the North Coast, and Ain Sokhna—over 10,000 units spanning approx. 5.5 million m², with numerous completed projects.
