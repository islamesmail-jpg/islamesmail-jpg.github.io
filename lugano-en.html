2 — The rise of AOG Developments

1) 18 years of achievement by the first generation — the founding father Ahmed Omar, founder of the economic group Lasirena Group.

2) In 2020, the second generation led by Mr. Mahmoud Omar joined with forward-looking ideas, establishing Manara Developments, which became a pioneer in Galala development.

3) In early 2025, the second generation leveraged significant expertise and financial strength to create a new equation by launching AOG Developments — to reshape the “real-estate formula”, delivering properties across classifications that reflect true value and restore investment feasibility and worth.

The new equation is choosing a property for its real value — a property by AOG Developments.
