1- About Lugano Al Galala

AOG Developments is led by entrepreneur Mr. Mahmoud Omar, a partner in several coastal development companies, most of which are delivered. In 2025, AOG enters with a strong land bank across multiple destinations:

  • Ain Sokhna
  • Ras Sedr
  • New Zayed
  • North Coast

2- AOG Background

1) 18 years of achievements driven by the first generation—founder Ahmed Omar of the economic group Lasirena Group.

2) In 2020, the second generation led by Mahmoud Omar joined with a forward vision, establishing Manara Developments, which became a leader in Galala development.

3) Early 2025, leveraging extensive expertise and strong financial capacity, the second generation launched AOG Developments to “reset the real-estate equation,” delivering properties that reflect their true value and restore investment returns.

The new equation: choose a property for its true value — an AOG property.
